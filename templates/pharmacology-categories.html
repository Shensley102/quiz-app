üìö Study Tips for Categorical Pharmacology

Studying drugs by category helps you:

  • Learn suffix patterns - Recognize drug classes by their endings (-pril, -olol, -dipine, -statin)
  • Understand mechanisms - See how drugs in the same class work similarly
  • Compare & contrast - Identify key differences between similar medications
  • Build systematic knowledge - Organize pharmacology by body system for better retention
  • Focus on weak areas - Target specific drug classes you find challenging
