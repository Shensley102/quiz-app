Pharmacology by Category

Targeted practice organized by drug class for systematic learning and mastery of medication categories

üí° Study Tips for Drug Classes

Learning medications by category helps you recognize patterns and remember key information. Look for:

  • Common suffixes (-pril for ACE inhibitors, -olol for beta blockers, -statin for cholesterol drugs)
  • Shared mechanisms of action within each class
  • Similar side effects across medications in the same category
  • Class-specific nursing considerations and patient education needs
