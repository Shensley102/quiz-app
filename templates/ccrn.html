üìö About the CCRN Exam

The CCRN (Critical Care Registered Nurse) certification validates your expertise in caring for acutely and critically ill adult patients. The actual exam consists of 150 questions covering clinical judgment across cardiovascular, pulmonary, endocrine, hematology, neurology, multisystem, and behavioral/psychosocial domains. These practice tests mirror the format and difficulty level of the real exam.
