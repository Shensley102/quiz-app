Study Tips for Success

  • Start with Multiple Choice: Build your foundation with guided questions before attempting fill-in-the-blank format.
  • Review Rationales: Read every rationale, even for correct answers, to deepen your understanding.
  • Use the Mastery System: Missed questions will reappear until you get them right consistently.
  • Track Your Progress: Monitor your first-try mastery percentage to identify weak areas.
  • Practice Frequently: Take shorter quizzes regularly rather than one long session for better retention.
  • Focus on Abnormalities: Pay special attention to abnormal values and nursing interventions.
