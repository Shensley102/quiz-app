How This Quiz Works

  • Keyboard Shortcuts: Press letter keys (A, B, C, D, etc.) to select/deselect answers, or press Enter to submit your answer.
  • Master Each Question: The quiz continues until you answer every question correctly on first try or after additional attempts.
  • Repeat Incorrect Answers: Questions you answer incorrectly will appear again later in the quiz for you to master.
  • Track Your Progress: The progress bar shows what percentage of questions you've mastered (answered correctly).
  • First-Try Accuracy: At the end, you'll see your first-try mastery percentage and total questions answered.
  • Review Your Performance: After completing the quiz, you'll see a detailed review of all questions with your answers and rationales.
  • Color-Coded Results: Questions are marked by difficulty (green = mastered, yellow/orange/red = more attempts needed).
  • Retry Missed Questions: After completing a quiz, you can retry only the questions you missed on first attempt for focused review.
